Full Webpage Screenshot: Privacy Policy

Full Webpage Screenshot captures a screenshot of the page you're currently viewing when you click its icon. That's the entire scope of what it does.

Full Webpage Screenshot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data about you, your browsing history, or the pages you capture. Everything happens locally in your browser. The resulting screenshot is saved directly to your own Downloads folder and never leaves your device.

Full Webpage Screenshot uses the Chrome DevTools Protocol (the debugger permission) solely to measure a page's full height and capture it in one image. It does not use this permission to inspect network traffic, read cookies, or monitor any other browser activity.

No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services of any kind are used by this extension.
